We Help the Military Community Fly the Benefit They've Earned.

Space-A travel offers free seats on military aircraft to service members, retirees, veterans, and their families. We watch official AMC terminal schedules around the clock and tell you the moment a seat shows up. So the benefit actually gets used.

07 / Respect, By Design

Some Things We Will Never Show.

FlySpaceA is built by people who served. That shapes what the tool does, and what it refuses to do.

OPSEC

No CENTCOM Flights.

Unlike others, we don't display CENTCOM flights. Out of respect for our service members, that region stays off every map and feed we publish. If you see CENTCOM Space-A flights posted anywhere, it's wrong.

Privacy

The Minimum Data, Nothing More.

We keep as little information as possible and treat data compliance as a standing commitment. Deletion requests are honored, every time.

Discipline

No Tail Numbers. Ever.

We never store or display tail numbers or aircraft identifiers. They're not in our database, so they can't leak from it.

08 / Why We Build

Built for the People Who Serve.

FlySpaceA is independent and disabled-veteran-owned. We built it out of pocket, with modern AI tooling, for one community: the service members, retirees, veterans, and families who fly Space-A.

We work closely with the Von Terra Project, a veteran-serving nonprofit, to put this capability in front of the people who earned it.

09 / The Way Forward

What Comes Next.

We have a clear plan, and it keeps the community first at every step.

  1. 01
